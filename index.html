ABOUT

Happy Plum Mandarin Learning offers individualized online language learning classes with a fun and flexible approach and proven results. We customize our topics so that each student remains engaged and eager to improve their speaking and listening skills. Using educational games, videos, books, and songs based on the age and interests of each individual, we make Mandarin achievable and accessible to students of all ages and learning styles, from absolute beginner to near-native fluency. Our positive, low pressure, and personalized approach build on each student’s success to foster an interest in language and culture awareness while increasing proficiency and maintaining interest and enthusiasm. We deliver high-quality education that fosters cultural and linguistic understanding in young Americans and want to spark lifelong curiosity in cultural and language learning.

What ages do you teach?

We teach ages 4-adult over zoom and all ages in person

What if my Child or I has already taken Chinese and we don't know what level we are at?

All of our teachers can assess the language level that you are at and adjust the content accordingly.

How long until I can hold a conversation? How do you measure students progress?

If you have 0 Mandarin background or exposure, you will see results within the first few lessons. Since we customize our content on age and learning style it is hard to gurantee that every child and student will progress at the same rate.

What is different about your program?

Our program has a Westernized approach. All of our classes are taught in english with a Mandarin component (can be immersion based if desired). Our classes are deisgned to be low pressure, fun, build confidence, and develop a love for learning a second language.

How many hours a week should I take class?

Almost all of our students do 30 min 2x per week. Classes are normally 1/2 hour, but we can also do an hour. Some students who are really into language learning do 2 hours per week.
