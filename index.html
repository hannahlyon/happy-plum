ABOUT

Happy Plum Mandarin Learning offers individualized online language learning classes with a fun and flexible approach and proven results. We customize our topics so that each student remains engaged and eager to improve their speaking and listening skills. Using educational games, videos, books, and songs based on the age and interests of each individual, we make Mandarin achievable and accessible to students of all ages and learning styles, from absolute beginner to near-native fluency. Our positive, low pressure, and personalized approach build on each student’s success to foster an interest in language and culture awareness while increasing proficiency and maintaining interest and enthusiasm. We deliver high-quality education that fosters cultural and linguistic understanding in young Americans and want to spark lifelong curiosity in cultural and language learning.
